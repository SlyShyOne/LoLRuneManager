*Installation:*
- Extract this in some folder, and drag import.exe to your taskbar.

*Usage:*
- Open a rune page in the client, and switch to grid view
- Go to RuneForge.gg or ProBuilds.net and copy a rune page (see https://imgur.com/a/IpeHz)
- Click on the program in the taskbar, don't touch the mouse while it's working.
